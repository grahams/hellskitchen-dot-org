1. ... Rc3
2. Rb3 Rxb3
3. h3 Rf3 mate.

This is an example of space and the mating net. Black builds a net around whites king, using the pawn chain f5-g4 and the black king on f6 to prevent escape. When black brings the rook down to c3 (instead of the worthless check on c4) and black completes the mating net. White has nowhere to go and no defense--- he's snared because he has no space. Black slides the rook to f3 for mate.
