Chess: The 7th rank is a MUTHAFUCK.
by Adam Fletcher

This position illustrates a 7th rank invasion as well as common promoting theme. In the endgame, pawns frequenty become the most important pieces because of their promoting potential. Here, both players seek to promote their advanced pawns and the game has become a race to the 8th rank. White, with a more advanced pawn and better tempo, plays:
1. Re7!
Pins the black pawn on g7 and defends the white pawn on d7. There's a mate threat here; see if you can find it before reading the rest of the line.
Black plays:
1. ... c2 looking for 2. ... c1=Q.
2. Nf6+ The rook on e7 helps the knight win the game. It's important to note that the knight not only checks the king but blocks the g8 escape square.
2. ... Kh8
3. Re8+ Rxe8 Check, force the rook to take, and...
4. dxe8=Q mate.
