I love dairy. Unfortunately for many Africans, the path of the dairy loving man is beset on all sides by the inequities of hypolactasia. Cursed is he who, after eating dairy, suffers cramps, bloating, and diarrhea. But the white man eats dairy as though with a great vengeance and furious anger.
You will know it is the white man's dairy when it lays its vengeance upon you....
