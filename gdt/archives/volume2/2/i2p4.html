Date: Thu, 14 Sep 1995 16:46:20
From: Marc

Dear GDT,

I couldn't help but notice that the default Netscape homepage setting for all the Macs was indeed Gracie's Dinnertime Theatre. So I said to myself, "It must be good if all these other people are reading it."
After surfing for a few hours on GDT and missing several unimportant liberal arts classes (senior seminar, etc...), I have come to the conclusion that GDT is in fact a quality publication. Who am I to judge, you may ask? Well, just some guy who was bored and got a kick out of GDT. Since I live and eat in Perkins (apts), I hadn't seen the hard copy version of GDT.
Anyway, before I miss a third consecutive class, I just thought I'd say that I really enjoyed GDT and will visit often. I will make a link to it on my (soon to be constructed) homepage. Keep up the good work and when's the next issue, dammit?
Marc
