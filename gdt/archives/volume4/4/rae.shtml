Random Acts of E-Mail
by Mark Nowak
You know I've always heard of the song "el condor pasa" by Simon and Garfunkel, but I never actually heard it till tonight. And it went something like this:
I'd rather be a mollusk than a shell,
If I could, if I would
I'd rather call it an odor than a smell,
If I could, yada yada yada
(meandering flute)

I waited 21 years for this?!? I'm SOOOO glad I don't remember much of the 70's.

Did I top myself?

--me, beautiful, me
