Random Facts
  • Thomas Parr of England was born in 1483 and died in 1635. King Charles I was so impressed with his age, he was invited to meet the king, but died during the trip.

  • Queen Christina of Sweden had a four inch cannon made for killing fleas in the 1600s.

  • There is no record of a flea ever being killed by one of the tiny cannon balls.
