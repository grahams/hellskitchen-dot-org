Hey Alanis, here's some irony for ya! In German, "erlich" means "honest". It so happens that one of Richard Nixon's top aides during the Watergate cover-up and media circus, responsible for helping feed the elder statesman† a daily diet of lies and denials, was named Erlichmann..."Honest man".

† What's the difference between a statesman and a politician? A statesman is a dead politician. Here's hoping for more statesmen!
