FAILURE

Mount: He's got one, but he doesn't know what it's named, because every time he gets close to it, it wallops him in the chest.
Friends: Apathy
Foes: Prometheus
Strength: none
Charisma: 3, He's even a failure at becoming a complete failure
Special Skills: Hind sight

Description: He looks like a "Disco Daddy," polyester everything. His cloths are so clinging that they fit themselves perfectly into the indents on his chest (which incidentally are upside down to drain out his luck). If Failure had a car it would be a Yugo.
