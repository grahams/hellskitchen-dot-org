by Troy Liston

The Martyr of the Week for Sept 29-Oct 5 is GDT's very own Troy Liston. Though it is undoubtedly the most popular weekly column in GDT, Troy hasn't received a single piece of fan mail for Martyr of the Week. Keeping with his interests, Troy has persevered and has, I daresay, earned the honor of Martyrdom for himself.
