A 100% Serious Editorial:
by Sean Hammond
We are now half-way through Volume 5 of Gracies Dinnertime Theatre, and I am being perfectly honest when I say that we are getting discouraged. The only letters of praise or hate that we have received came from a fan in Texas who stumbled upon our web site over the summer and an alumni who shares our distaste for the Reporter. I am reprinting them in the hopes that others may follow.
Maybe you didn't realize it, but the staff of Gracies Dinnertime Theatre is composed of only volunteers. There is no payment, other than the feedback we receive. When we ask a survey, we really want responses: we do keep track and have a running tally, even if the questions seem silly. If we made a mistake, either in our facts or in our layout...tell us. We're not the Reporter. When we say we're willing to give away a free GDTee shirt to anyone who can come up with a topic we can't write about, we mean it. And don't think someone else will write to us because, let me tell you, it's not happening.
We realize that this editorial is the literary equivalent of asking for a sympathy-fuck, but we need the support right now. Let us know what you think, for better or for worse.
