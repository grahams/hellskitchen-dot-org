Random Facts
  • The British Parliamentary Papers (1800-1900) comes in 1,100 volumes, weighs 3.5 tons and costs $65,000 per set.
    There are only 500 copies in existence.

  • British Historian Thomas May (1595-1650) tried to hide his immense drooping chin by tying strips of cloth around his head to hold it up. Unfortunately, the cloth was not loose enough to allow him to swallow his food and died of choking/strangulation.

  • Only members of Congress are allowed to borrow books from (duh) the Library of Congress. Over 300,000 books have been missing for so long that they are considered stolen.
