Army of Darkness
by Kelly Gunter
FRUSTRATION
Mount: Anxiety
Charisma: He's got friends, but only because they're afraid to be his enemy.
Strength: when need be
Meditation: 0
Endurance: "I don't know how much more of this she can take, captain!"
Description: Frustration's face seems as if it's locked in a terminal grin; not a friendly sort of grin. He is forever on the edge; any given person to encounter him will have pretty even chances that he will either commit a spontaneous act of violence, or a menacing act of kindness (the type of kindness you want to keep as far away from as possible). He looks as though every muscle in his body is going through a twenty-four hour-a-day charlie horse, You almost feel the urge to teach him lamaz, to help him through the tough times.
