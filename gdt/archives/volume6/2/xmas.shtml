RIT's Twelve Days of Christmas
To be sung to the tune of (duh) "The twelve days of Christmas"
by Mary Huguenor, Mindy Proscia, Tim Miller, and Adam Miles
On the first day of Christmas, a RITard gave to me...
A HUNDRED-THOUSAND DOLLAR DEGREE

On the second day of Christmas, a RITard gave to me...
TWO ROOMMATES YELLING,
AND A HUNDRED-THOUSAND DOLLAR DEGREE.

On the third day of Christmas, a RITard gave to me...
THREE DRAGQUEENS KISSING,
