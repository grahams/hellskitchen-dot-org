DEAR BFG:
HOW MUCH WOOD COULD A WOODCHUCK CHUCK, IF A WOODCHUCK COULD CHUCK WOOD?

Dear Sir,

A woodchuck could chuck as much wood as a woodchuck would chuck if a woodchuck could chuck wood. In short, seven logs. Next question...?
-BFG

DEAR BFG:

IT'S ISTANBUL, NOW CONSTANTINOPLE. NOW IT'S ISTANBUL, NOW CONSTANTINOPLE. BEEN A LONG TIME GONE, CONSTANTINOPOLE. WHY DID CONSTANTINOPLE GET THE WORKS?

Dear Other Sir,

That's nobody's business but the Turks. Besides, nobody ever seems to lament the passing of Byzantium.
-BFG
