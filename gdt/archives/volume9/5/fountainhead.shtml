Deep within Hell's Kitchen, NYC, Fountainhead draws pure, natural water from the Wynand Building's private reserve. Here, for more than 50 years Fountainhead has been naturally filtered through 500 feet of concrete and bedrock. Bottled at the source, nothing is added. It's water the way Howard would have wanted it to be.
