Donland Developer Behind Gates Pie Gag

As tension escalates over the Microsoft-Donland lawsuit, a software developer in Donland has claimed responsibility for the recent whipped cream pie attack on Microsoft's CEO Bill Gates in Brussels this past week. The clandestine developer claims he hired professional Belgian pie-thrower Noel Godin to throw several pies in the face of Gates during the return trip from Switzerland. "I wish I could have been there to see the look on his face when those pies hit him," the developer said in a phone interview, following up with a squeal of laughter and a snort.

The Donland government denies any involvement in the matter. Microsoft had no comment, but did note that Gates usually prefers Key Lime.
