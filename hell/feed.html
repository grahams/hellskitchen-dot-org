General Info.

The main branch of Hell's Kitchen is located in Rochester, NY.

Hell's Kitchen is a non-profit organization (501(c)(3)) and depends on contributions from businesses and fund raisers. As of now, it's still possible to order subscriptions to the members of Hell's Kitchen in Rochester, NY.

If you just have something to say, that's cool too. We love to hear from our visitors...be it fan mail, hate mail, suggestions, whatever.

So, if you have something to share, or want to let us know how you feel about something one of the member groups has done, feel free to get in touch with us.
