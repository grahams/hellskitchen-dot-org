Undiscovered Band of the Month

Written By Chi Chi

- Johnny Socko -

Johnny Socko is an amazing ska/punk/polka band from Indianapolis, Indiana. They have been together for six years and have made three albums: Bovaquarium, Ooh, I Do Hope It's Roast Beef, and Full Trucker Effect. The latter is music from and inspired by the 70s trucking movie of the same title, and is extremely good. The band has a great sense of humor, as found in such songs as vasectomy. They are also really friendly and great guys to talk to if you get a chance. I highly recommend seeing them live, as they put on a great show. From onstage dress that can range anywhere from a white trash T-shirt (It's not a Beer Gut - It's a Fuel Tank for a Sex Machine) to dressing in drag, this band isn't afraid of being too bold or out of hand. As long they keep making such classics as "Bitch Stole My Hat," Johnny Socko is a definite keeper.
