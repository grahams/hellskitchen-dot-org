Undiscovered Band of the Month

Written By Chi Chi

- The Pilfers -

The Pilfers are a new, five-piece, all-star ska band. Coolie Ranx, formerly of the Toasters, delivers energetic vocals for the group. He is supported by Vinny Nobile (formerly of Bim Skala Bim) on trombone, Anna Milat-Meyer on bass, Nick Bacon (from the Drivers), and James Blanck (from Skinnerbox). Hailing from all over the Northeast, these guys have brought forth a very unique mix of traditional and third-wave sounds that is truly all their own. They currently have one self-titled album out, one that I particularly recommend. Currently playing across the northeast with the likes of the Pietasters, Scofflaws, and Bim Skala Bim, don't miss the opportunity to see them if they come to your town.

You can visit their homepage at www.pilfers.com These guys kick ass.
