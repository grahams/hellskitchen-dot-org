Rules for the Pig-Guessing Contest

    1. Only one guess per name per household per county.

    2. You can use only real words. Complex words, meaning real words added to or multiplied by i, the square root of -1, will not be accepted.

    3. The word "Pig" may not appear out of context, as well as the words "pie", "donut", or "Feliz Navidad".

    5. All submissions in english please.

    6. Entries may be recorded to monitor service.

    7. E-boy, or any other member of the Piggy Lovin' staff, or their families, loved ones, pets, houses, or appliances (which can be made to include aquaintances as well) are not eligable to receive prizes.
